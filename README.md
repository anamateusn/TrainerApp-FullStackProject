## Project Overview

TrainerApp is a full-stack platform designed to support trainers in managing their daily professional activities.  
The goal was to centralize classroom organization, administrative requirements, financial tracking, and logistics into one intuitive system.

The platform covers:

- Classroom tools (student lists, materials, grades)
- Logistical management (institutions, courses, modules)
- Administrative documents (legal requirements for trainers in Portugal)
- Financial management (invoicing, earnings, revenue comparison)

This project was built entirely by the team, covering **database planning**, **frontend development**, and **backend programming**.

---

## Client Requirements & Objectives

The client requested a platform that was **easy and intuitive** while being **complete and reliable**.

Key requirements included:

### **Financial Tracking**
- Total invoicing
- Effective earnings
- Visual comparison of revenue between institutions

### **Administrative Management**
- Upload multiple legal documents (insurance, criminal record, ID)
- Merge and download documents in a single file

### **Calendar**
- Simple and intuitive use
- Color distinction by course, module, or institution

### **Classroom Management**
- Student registration and final grade assignment
- Management of courses, institutions, and modules

## Challenges & Limitations

- The initial database model required several updates and new tables, causing integration delays.
- Limited access to the client led to some decisions being made based on assumptions from early meetings.
- PDF conversion and merged download required restructuring and additional logic.
- Search and edit features were complex due to pre-selecting related entities.
- FullCalendar integration demanded CSS and JavaScript adjustments due to lack of prior experience.
